FAQ

How do I prepare?

Hydrate, sleep well, avoid alcohol for 24 hours, eat a solid meal, and wear comfortable clothing that exposes the area.

Do you do cover-ups?

Yes — we’ll review your current piece, skin tone, placement, and goals to propose a tasteful solution.

Aftercare?

Remove wrap as directed, wash gently with fragrance-free soap, pat dry, apply thin ointment, avoid sun and soaking for 2 weeks.

Can I bring a friend?

One support person is fine for consults. For sessions, we keep the floor focused and sanitary.
